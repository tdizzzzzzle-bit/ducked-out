About

The story behind the ducks.

Hand-drawn, hand-pressed, shipped with love.

Duck & Cover started with a simple idea: draw weird ducks, put them on shirts. Every design is drawn by hand with colored pencils — no AI, no stock art, no shortcuts.

Each shirt is pressed in-house using DTF transfers on premium Bella+Canvas blanks. That means every order is made with care, not churned out by a factory.

The ducks have names. They have personalities. Gerald wears roller skates and a party hat. Bernard is a horse that is also a duck. Sid is a punk rock ice cream cone. They don't explain themselves, and neither do we.
